Origin A typeface born at Flutgraben
Display

Rhizotype is a variable display typeface developed by and for Flutgraben. Its construction logic is geometric and modular — influenced by Bauhaus and Futura — but the visual result is organic and material.

Characters are built from atomic circular elements whose edges dissolve outward, suggesting connection and trans-individuality rather than isolated, self-contained forms.

Use Variable axes
WGHT · SPOT · RECT

Three variable axes: weight, spot density, and rectangular variant. The typeface is used across Flutgraben's visual identity — website, print, and event materials.
